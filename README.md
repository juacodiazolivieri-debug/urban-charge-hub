# Urban Charge Hub - Montevideo

## 🚀 Innovation in Sustainable Electric Mobility
We are transforming underutilized urban infrastructure (such as former security booths) into fast-charging hubs for personal electric mobility vehicles (scooters, electric bicycles).

## 🏗️ The Problem
The lack of strategic and accessible charging points is a critical barrier to the adoption of clean transportation in dense urban environments.

## ⚡ The Solution
We use Artificial Intelligence (Gemini API) to optimize the location and management of charging nodes based on:
* Real-time traffic flow.
* Availability of existing infrastructure.
* Projected energy demand.

## 🤖 Technologies Used
* **Google Gemini API**: For predictive analysis of energy demand.
* **Python**: Main programming language for system logic.
* **Sustainable Mobility Ecosystem**: Modular infrastructure adapted for urban needs.
